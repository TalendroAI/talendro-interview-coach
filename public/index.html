Voice Interview Session

Practice speaking with your AI interviewer

Follow These Steps

1

Your Résumé

Open your résumé file (Word, PDF, Google Doc, or any text document). Select ALL the text (Ctrl+A on Windows, Cmd+A on Mac), copy it (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C), then paste it (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) into the "Your Résumé" box on the left.

2

Job Description

Go to the job posting webpage. Select ALL the job description text, copy it, then paste it into the "Job Description" box on the left.

3

Company Website

Type or paste the company's website URL (e.g., https://company.com) into the "Company Website" field on the left. This helps personalize your interview.

4

Save Your Information

Click the "Save Documents" button on the left. Wait for it to turn green confirming your information is saved.

5

Begin Your Interview

Click the "Start Interview" button that appears on the left. Allow microphone access when your browser asks. Your AI interviewer will greet you and begin.

6

Finish & Get Results

When your interview is complete, click "Complete Session" on the left. You'll receive your transcript and feedback via email.

💡 Pro Tip Use headphones for the best audio experience. Find a quiet space where you can speak freely.
