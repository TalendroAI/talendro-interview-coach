Welcome to Interview Coach

1 Open your résumé file, press Ctrl+A (Mac: ⌘+A) to select all, then Ctrl+C (Mac: ⌘+C) to copy, then Ctrl+V (Mac: ⌘+V) to paste into the Résumé box
2 Go to the job posting, select all the text, copy it, and paste it into the Job Description box
3 Type or paste the company's website URL
4 Click "Start My Interview Prep" — the button will unlock once steps 1-2 are done
5 Once the Quick Prep interview content is displayed on screen, click "Complete Session" to receive your full results via email
